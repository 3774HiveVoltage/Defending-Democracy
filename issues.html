Issues

The topics we focus on

These issue areas guide the work we feature and create space for youth to take concrete action and lead local change.

Environmentalism

Protect land, clean streets, and promote sustainable civic projects.

Voting

Mobilize young people, host voter education drives, and strengthen turnout.

Gender Equality

Support inclusive leadership and equal opportunity for all genders.

Racial Equality

Build solidarity, confront bias, and invest in equitable policy change.

Civil Rights

Defend free expression, protest rights, and access to fair representation.

Education

Create resources that teach civic engagement and democratic values.
